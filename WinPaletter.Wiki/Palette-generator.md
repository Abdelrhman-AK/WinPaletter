## Introduction

If you're unsure about which colors to use for your Windows theme, WinPaletter can help by generating a color palette from a single color or from an image.

---

## 1. Generate a Palette from an Image

### 1. Click the Toolbar Button

![Toolbar Button](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/0.png?raw=true)

### 2. Select "Generate a palette from an image"

![Generate from Image](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/1_0.png?raw=true)

### 3. Choose Image Source

You can use your current wallpaper or pick any image from your device.

![Choose Image](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_0.png?raw=true)

### 4. Adjust Options

Customize the maximum number of colors, quality, and whether to ignore white colors.

![Options](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_1.png?raw=true)

### 5. Tune Color Degrees

Adjust color degrees if you need to improve accessibility with your current Windows appearance.

![Color Degrees](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_2.png?raw=true)

### 6. View the Generated Palette

See the palette created from your selected image.

![Palette Result](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_3.png?raw=true)

### 7. Apply the Palette

You can randomly assign the generated palette to your Windows colors, as shown below:

![Distribute Palette](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_4.png?raw=true)

![Distribute Palette Example](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_6.png?raw=true)

### 8. Action Buttons

![Action Buttons](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/2_5.png?raw=true)

- **Done**: Close the window and keep the new palette.
- **Cancel**: Close the window and restore your previous palette.

## 2. Generate a Palette from a Color

### 1. Click the Toolbar Button

![Toolbar Button](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/0.png?raw=true)

### 2. Select "Generate a palette from a color"

![Generate from Color](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/1_1.png?raw=true)

### 3. Choose Your Color and Settings

Pick your base color, set the minimum number of colors, and choose whether to include inverted shades for more variety.

![Choose Color](https://github.com/Abdelrhman-AK/WinPaletter/blob/master/WinPaletter.wiki/Asset/PaletteGenerator/3_0.png?raw=true)

### 4. Tune Color Degrees
